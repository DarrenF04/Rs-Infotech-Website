About Rs Infotech

R.S. Infotech is one of the best institutes for technical education in Vasai. We offer professional training for high-in-demand jobs at affordable charges. From Graphic Designing & Visual Effects to Advanced MS Excel & Cyber Security, our advanced curriculums cover wide-spectrum of study areas that fill in the skill gaps in the corporate world.

The main thought behind establishing this institute is to go beyond giving generic training to the students it is to prepare them to face the corporate world and help them fit easily into their roles as professionals. We offer courses that are career-oriented and makes our students job-ready right after graduating from the institute!
